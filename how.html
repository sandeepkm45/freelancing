Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using FreelanceHub

How much does it cost to join FreelanceHub?
Creating an account on FreelanceHub is completely free for both clients and freelancers.
Can I hire freelancers for long-term projects?
Absolutely! FreelanceHub supports both short-term projects and long-term engagements. For ongoing work, you can set up milestone payments or hourly contracts. Many successful client-freelancer relationships on our platform evolve into long-term partnerships.
How do I know a freelancer is qualified?
Every freelancer on our platform has a detailed profile showing their skills, portfolio, work history, and client reviews. Many freelancers also complete skill assessments and verification processes. You can review all this information before making a hiring decision to ensure they're the right fit for your project.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
If you're not satisfied with the delivered work, you can request revisions based on the original project requirements. Communication is key - most issues can be resolved through clear feedback. If necessary, our dispute resolution team can mediate to find a fair solution for both parties.
